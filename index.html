About Me

Hello, People. Let me tell you a little bit about myself. I have many qualifications, most of which can hardly be attained by mere mortals. Not only am I the Assistant to the Regional Manager AND Assistant Regional Manager of a very well-known paper company, but I am also the owner of the building in which that same well-known paper company is housed. In addition to my highly-coveted office titles (none of which Jim currently holds) I am also the proud owner of Scranton's one and only BEET FARM! That's right...Beets...and we have plenty of 'em. My cousin Mose and I run the farm as well as a very nice Bed and Breakfast business, located right on our property. For recommendations feel free to contact one of our previous guests, Jim and/or Pam Halpert. I'm quite sure their phone numbers are listed.

If you happen to be in the Scranton area and are in the market for either paper or beets, contact me either by phone or by submitting a form on the "Contact" page of this website. I look forward to doing business with you! P.S. I am also a Karate instructor and am accepting new students on Tuesday and Thursday evenings at the Electric City DOJO, located next door to the Electric City Sewage Company.
