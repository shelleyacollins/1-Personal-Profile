About Me

Hello! My name is Shelley. Here's a fun fact: Although I have never stepped foot into a Latin class, I have suddenly attained an amazing skill... I can come up with a zillion words in Latin just by tapping a few keys and hitting the Enter button! Fantasic, I know. Hopefully I'll be able to use this simple trick, along with many other newly attained skills and knowledge to one day become a Web Developer. Why Web Development? I'm glad you asked. I couldn't think of any other career paths that would allow me to use my drive towards fixing broken things (problem-solving skills), my inability to take "no" for an answer if I believe there might be a solution out there somewhere (tenacity), and finally, my desire to do something that benefits the lives of others. Simply put, I look forward to working with other like-minded individuals to provide innovative, creative, web-based tools and products that simplify and bring value to the lives of others. The rest, my new friends, is Latin.
